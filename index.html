what is drop in?

Drop In is a FREE iOS mobile app that manages a visit-based rewards program.
Stay at one of our partner bars with Bluetooth on to receive free rewards for future visits.

Download today and #DropIn

why use drop in?

  • free stuff? yes please.

    Free drinks, food discounts & other perks pushed to your iPhone after visiting a venue.

  • earn a reward each visit

    Receive one reward after every visit and additional promos as you continue to #DropIn.

  • Easy reward redemption

    Double tap the reward and show your bartender/server. Don't forget to tip!

  • effortless

    No need to check-in. Drop In automatically connects you to the bar when you walk in.

  • low-energy bluetooth

    Enabling Bluetooth is required, but we use a low energy signal with minimal effect on your battery.

  • support local venues

    All the fun is happening in your backyard. Grab your friends and #DropIn!

how drop in works.

Download on the Apple App Store for FREE and sign up. Enter a promo code & receive bonus rewards to get you started!

Turn on Bluetooth & visit a Drop In partner bar. Stay for the time specified (30-45 min depending on venue) to earn a reward that can be redeemed during your next visit.

Easily redeem rewards earned on your device by double tapping & showing your local bartender/server. Don't forget to tip!

Continue to earn rewards at partner bars each time you visit. You will also receive special promos from the bar as you visit multiple times.
